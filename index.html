About
Myself

Born June 23rd, 1996, I'm a passionate graphic designer who recently completed my studies at the Lisbon School of Design. My professional journey began in hospitality — 10 years in the field — before I made the decision to pursue my true calling in the creative industry. Looking for a new challenge, I now build visual identities, print work, and campaigns that are bold, coherent, and impossible to ignore.
